I'm looking for MS/PhD positions for Fall 2024!
I am currently a Research Associate at the Robotics Research Center (RRC), IIIT Hyderabad advised by Prof. Madhava Krishna. Previously, I was a research intern at IIT Bombay and IIT Guwahati. Apart from research, I have participated in national and international robotics competitions. I completed my Bachelors from Manipal Institute of Technology in Mechatronics

My research interest revolves around motion planning for robots and decision-making for AI agents. I broadly focus on enhancing the adaptability of learned planners through predefined environment dynamics or learned environment models. For example, demonstrating skills like holding a cup of tea, pushing a book, folding clothes, or simply avoiding collisions requires diverse training data to satisfy the constraints for each skill.

I am mainly interested in developing algorithms that can learn from prior experience and promptly adapt to novel tasks and environmental constraints, relying primarily on real-time observations during inference. The ultimate objective is to create versatile robotic agents capable of aiding humans in need, executing repetitive tasks, and functioning in hazardous environments.

I enjoy traveling to new cities, trying new cuisine, and visiting hill stations! (Travel blog coming soon)
